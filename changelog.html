Changelog¶

dev¶

  • qop convert [...] --remove-art removes album art from files. Use qop convert ... --convert-none --remove-art to only remove album covers without transcoding any files.

  • qop convert now preserves media tags when transcoding audio formats; thanks to mediafile

  • qop progress now displays active tasks together with the progress bar

  • Prototype that lays out the basic architecture and implements the most important core features
